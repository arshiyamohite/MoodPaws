What we detect
Three core emotional states in dogs and cats
😌
Calm
Relaxed posture, soft eyes, neutral ears, tail held naturally. Your pet feels safe and comfortable in their environment.
😰
Anxious
Tucked tail, flattened ears, lowered body, avoiding eye contact. Signs of stress, fear, or an unfamiliar situation.
🐾
Playful
Play bow, upright tail, bright eyes, bouncy movement. Your pet is engaged, social, and ready for interaction.
😤
Aggressive
Stiff posture, direct stare, raised hackles, tense jaw. Your pet feels threatened or is asserting dominance — give space immediately.
😴
Tired
Droopy eyes, slow movements, low-energy posture, seeking rest. Your pet needs quiet time to recharge.
